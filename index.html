FAQ

Soapbox is an app that allows mobile users to e-volunteer. That is, just as you check into various mobile apps throughout your day, you check into Soapbox once a day for about a minute (we can even send you a reminder). The only difference though is that your time spent on Soapbox each day is money straight to the charity of your choice. It’s that easy.
